## 📌 Sample Insights

Some key observations from the analysis include:

* A significant portion of tech workers (41% to 48%) report experiencing high or severe burnout symptoms monthly.
* Females show a higher average burnout score (5.52 vs. 5.34 for males) and a higher burnout rate (69% vs. 56%).
* Daily use of AI tools (by 73% of workers) correlates with increased anxiety (higher GAD-7 scores), with 77% of employees reporting AI adds to their workload.
* Despite limited access (only 34% have employer therapy access), using therapy significantly reduces depression scores (1.92-point drop in PHQ-9).
